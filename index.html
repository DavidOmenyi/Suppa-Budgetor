    How to Use Suppa Budgetor

    Welcome to your personal, fully offline financial tracker. Because this app runs entirely inside your device's browser, your data is 100% private.

    1. Setting Budgets

    • Go to the Budget tab to set your monthly targets.
    • You can set a budget for an entire Category (e.g., Food), or leave the Expense Name blank to act as a general bucket.
    • Budgets automatically roll forward to future months.

    2. Smart Log Entry

    • In the Log Entry tab, you can manually type transactions, or use the 🤖 Smart Message Paste box.
    • Simply copy an SMS from M-Pesa, Airtel Money, or your Bank and paste it into the box. The app will automatically extract the vendor, amount, and date!
    • If the app detects a transaction fee in the SMS, it will ask if you want to automatically log that as a separate expense.

    3. Custom Shopping Lists

    • Go to the Lists 🛒 tab to draft shopping lists before you go to the store.
    • Add items with their estimated prices and quantities to track your expected total.
    • One-Click Buy: While shopping, tap the green ✓ Buy button next to an item. It will be removed from your list and instantly logged into your main Ledger as an actual Expense!

    4. Portfolio & Savings Tracking

    • Initial Funds: In the Log Entry tab, select Type: Savings (Portfolio) > Action: Opening Account Balance. Type your account name. This safely establishes your initial portfolio balance.
    • Saving Money: Log a Deposit. This registers as an expense (reducing unassigned cash) and adds money to the Portfolio!
    • Spending Savings: When you spend saved money, first log a Withdrawal (moves money from portfolio back to cash), then log your actual purchase as a standard Expense.

    5. Viewing & Editing Data

    • The Summaries tab breaks down your performance with dynamic filters.
    • To Edit or Delete: In the Summaries tab, scroll down to the Transactions Log or click any Expense Name to open the detailed ledger. Click Edit or Del to modify records.

    6. Backups & Data Safety (Crucial!)

    • Because this app is "Local-First", your data lives exclusively in your browser's local storage. If you clear your browser history or cache, you will lose everything.
    • Go to the Utilities 🛠️ tab to Export (💾) your data regularly.
    • Your custom Portfolio accounts and Opening Balances are safely packed inside your main Transactions backup CSV.
    • To restore your data on a new device or browser, simply click the Import (📥) buttons and select your saved CSV files.
